# Privacy Policy — SC Blackboard Assistant

**Last updated:** March 9, 2026

## Data Collection

SC Blackboard Assistant collects **website content** (text, links, and documents) exclusively from the Schwarzman College Blackboard LMS (`lms.sc.tsinghua.edu.cn`) to build a searchable index of your course materials.

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally in your browser** using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

- Indexed resource titles, URLs, and descriptions
- Extracted text content from HTML pages and PDFs
- Your API key for the chosen AI provider
- Chat history and user preferences (theme, settings)

**No data is sent to any server operated by us.** There is no backend, no analytics, and no tracking.

## Third-Party Services

When you submit a query, your question and relevant content snippets are sent to the AI provider you selected (OpenAI, Anthropic, or DeepSeek) to generate an answer. This communication is made directly from your browser to the provider's API. We do not proxy, log, or store these requests.

Please review your chosen provider's privacy policy:

- [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- [DeepSeek Privacy Policy](https://www.deepseek.com/privacy)

## Data Sharing

We do not collect, transmit, or share any user data with any party.

## Data Deletion

All data can be deleted at any time from the extension's Settings panel (Clear Index, Clear Chat). Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.
